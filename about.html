About us

Built from friendship, discipline, and a belief in chess education.

BruvsChess Educational Services grew from an early dream to take chess into schools and help young people think with more focus, patience, and confidence.

Our beginning

From BruvsChess Academy to BruvsChess Educational Services.

The work behind BruvsChess began around 2007-2008 as BruvsChess Academy, an idea started by Ayodele Atejide and Fawale John Oyeuyemi. They were friends who regarded each other as brothers, and that sense of brotherhood shaped the early vision: use chess as a serious educational tool for children and schools.

In those early years, the work grew through persistence. Atejide pursued school opportunities in Lagos, while Fawale did the same in Osun State. They visited schools, shared proposals, and kept building the idea long before the structure became formal.

After years of conversations, refinement, and growth, the education service was formally registered in Abuja in 2019. Today, BruvsChess carries that same founding spirit forward through school chess programs, private lessons, evaluation sessions, and state-level program support.
