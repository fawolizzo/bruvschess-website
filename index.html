Our teaching method

Structured lessons, measurable growth, and plenty of play.

We blend instruction, practice, reflection, and friendly competition so students can understand ideas deeply and use them confidently.

  1. Assess Place students at the right level through simple games and puzzle checks.
  2. Teach Introduce one clear concept per session with examples students can remember.
  3. Practice Use guided positions, pair play, clocks, notation, and review boards.
  4. Celebrate Track progress through certificates, rankings, exhibitions, and events.
